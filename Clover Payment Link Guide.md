# Sending a Payment Link — Zukerino Pastry Shop (Clover Station Duo)

## The honest situation

The **Clover Station Duo cannot create or send a payment link by itself.** Sending a pay link / invoice is a web feature in Clover, not a function built into the countertop device, and the Station Duo has no general web browser to get around it. ("Send invoices directly from the terminal" is a feature merchants have requested from Clover for years — it still isn't built.)

The good news: the owner does **not** need to sit at a back-office computer. The fix is a **phone or tablet kept at the register**, signed into the same Clover account. All payments still land in the one Clover account and report alongside counter sales.

---

## Recommended: Clover Go on a phone/tablet at the counter

This is the closest thing to "doing it from the terminal" — a handheld that lives by the register.

**One-time setup**
1. On a phone or tablet, install **Clover Go** (Apple App Store or Google Play).
2. Sign in with the shop's existing Clover login. (No extra card reader is needed just to send invoices.)

**To send a payment link (every time)**
1. Tap **New Sale**.
2. Add the items the customer ordered (or skip to a custom amount).
3. Tap **Take payment** → **More options** → **Send as an invoice**.
4. Tap **Add customer** and enter their **email** (or pick a saved customer).
5. Confirm the amount and any note, then set the **payment due date** — choose **Upon Receipt** for pay-now.
6. Review the invoice and tap **Send invoice** → **Done**.

The customer gets an email with a secure link to pay by card. The payment appears in the same Clover account as your in-store sales.

---

## If it must literally run on the Station Duo's own screen

Native Clover can't, but you can check the **Clover App Market** (the **More Tools** app on the device) for a **third-party invoicing / payment-request app** that installs on the Station. A few exist. Caveats: they're third-party, usually carry a monthly fee, and quality varies — confirm with your Clover merchant rep before relying on one.

---

## Fallback (no app install)

From any phone or computer browser, log in to the **Clover Dashboard** → **Virtual Terminal** → **New Sale** → **Invoice**, pick the customer, choose **Itemized invoice** (menu items) or **Quick charge** (flat amount), and **Send Invoice**. Same result as Clover Go — just in a browser instead of the app.

---

### Bottom line

| Want | Reality |
|---|---|
| Send a pay link tapping the Station Duo itself | Not possible natively; only via a third-party App Market app |
| Send a pay link standing at the counter, no PC | **Clover Go on a phone/tablet** — recommended |
| Send a pay link from anywhere | Clover Dashboard → Virtual Terminal in a browser |
